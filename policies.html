Store Policies

The policies below explain how we handle returns, support, custom builds, delivery, and fraud prevention at The Computer Shop.

1. Returns, Exchanges, and Refunds

Unopened products in their original sealed condition may be eligible for return or exchange after inspection, provided the product, packaging, accessories, manuals, seals, and serial numbers are all intact and the item remains in original sale condition.

Opened products may not be eligible for direct return or exchange where the product cannot be restored to the condition in which it was sold, where seals or packaging have been broken in a way that affects resale as new, or where the condition of the product changed due to customer use, handling, or damage.

All return, exchange, and refund requests are subject to inspection before approval.

2. Defective or Incorrect Products

If a product is defective, not working properly, or does not match what was agreed upon, the customer may request inspection and review.

Claims related to defects, faults, or incorrect products are handled after diagnostic inspection and verification. If the issue is confirmed and is not caused by misuse, tampering, accidental damage, liquid damage, electrical damage, or unauthorized modification, the customer may be offered the appropriate solution, which may include repair, replacement, exchange, or refund depending on the case.

3. Warranty and Lifetime Troubleshooting

We offer free lifetime troubleshooting and diagnosis for devices and components bought from our store, including both hardware and software inspection.

This service includes checking, diagnosing, and troubleshooting issues. It does not include the cost of replacement parts once the relevant warranty period has expired.

Warranty-related claims may be refused where inspection shows misuse, physical abuse, liquid damage, electrical damage, altered or missing serial numbers, tampering, or unauthorized repair attempts.

4. Repeated Issues

If the same material issue continues to appear after repair or support attempts and it substantially affects normal use, the case will be reviewed again and handled accordingly.

5. Custom Build Policy

Custom-built systems are assembled according to the customer’s approved specifications.

Before assembly or part ordering begins, the customer will be asked to confirm the final configuration. Once a custom build has been approved and parts have been ordered, assembled, or allocated specifically for that order, cancellation, return, or refund options may become limited depending on the stage of the order.

If a custom-built system contains a faulty component or does not match the approved specifications, the issue will be handled through inspection and review.

6. Anti-Tampering and Inspection Policy

To protect both the customer and our store, we record serial numbers for major components and may document product condition, packaging, and accessories before dispatch and during return intake.

Returns, exchanges, warranty claims, or refunds may be refused where inspection shows serial-number mismatch, removed or altered labels, missing accessories or packaging, physical tampering, opened heatsinks or coolers, changed thermal pads, missing screws, bent pins, liquid residue, burn marks, internal component substitution, or other signs of misuse or fraud.

No refund, replacement, or warranty approval is finalized until inspection is completed.

7. Delivery and Acceptance

Customers must provide accurate delivery details, including city, building number, floor number where applicable, apartment number where applicable, and Google Plus Code where requested.

Visible shipping damage, missing items, or delivery-condition disputes should be reported as soon as possible after receipt.

For high-value orders, customers are strongly encouraged to keep the original packaging and record an unboxing video in case a shipping or missing-item dispute arises.

Delivery dates are estimates unless explicitly confirmed otherwise.

8. Order Verification and Fraud Prevention

We reserve the right to verify customer identity, phone number, address, and order details for high-value or suspicious transactions.

We may cancel, delay, or refuse orders in cases of suspected fraud, failed payment verification, stock unavailability, clear pricing error, abusive behavior, repeated false claims, abusive chargebacks, or other conduct that places the transaction at risk.

If an order is canceled before dispatch, any amount due for refund will be returned through the appropriate payment channel.

9. Abuse of Returns, Warranty, or Support

Fraudulent or abusive conduct, including false defect claims, false missing-item claims, return fraud, component swapping, serial-number tampering, or repeated misuse of support channels, may result in refusal of future sales or services and may be escalated to the relevant courier, payment provider, manufacturer, or other appropriate authority where needed.

10. Final Note

These policies are intended to keep our process clear, fair, and secure for everyone involved. If you have any questions about an order, a return, a warranty issue, or a custom build, please contact us directly before taking further action.
